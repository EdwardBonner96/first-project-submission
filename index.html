About Us

Didcot Film Club is a friendly, local film club that has been gathering together to watch and review films since 2018. We welcome anyone with a strong passion for film, theater and reviewing films. The purpose of Didcot Film Club (DFC) is to enhance a members understanding and knowledge of films, both well known and underappreciated. We have a fun and lighthearted environment for film enthusiasts that like to look a little deeper into film and cinema. After viewing we have regular discussions and optional debates in which everyone can express their opinion and thoughts so we can discover the message the film was trying to establish, whether it's a deep-rooted philosophical discussion on human nature, politics or if that really was a simple surface-level film to enjoy for what it seemed to be. If this club sounds like something you'd enjoy then please sign up below or come to one of our meet-ups and get to know us! Feel free to come along with any of your own suggestions or favourite films and if you have any questions just sign up below and we'll get in touch with you as soon as we can. It's always better to get in touch since we can be sure to book seats by eachother and regularly get discounts from local venues!
